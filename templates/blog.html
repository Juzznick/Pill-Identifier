Blog

Presented a paper titled "An Accurate Deep Learning-Based Pill Detection with Intelligent Medicinal Drug Identification System" at the 4th International Conference on Advanced Information Science and Computing Systems (ICAISCS). This was organised by the Department of Computer Science Applications, Christ Nagar College, Maranalloor, Thiruvananthapuram in collaboration with the International Centre for Free and Open Source Software (ICFOSS) and the Computer Society of India (CSI) on 24th January 2025.


We presented our project at Innofest 2025, a prestigious project expo organized by Vidya Academy of Science and Technology. This platform enabled young innovators to showcase their creative ideas and technical skills. Our project garnered valuable feedback from faculty, industry experts, and fellow students, which helped us refine and validate our work further. It was a fantastic opportunity to network, learn, and connect with a vibrant community of budding engineers and innovators.
